Программа тура
Arrival in Tashkent, the capital of Uzbekistan.
Warm reception guide and driver.
Transfer to the hotel.
09:00 start briefing introduced by guide.
At 10:00 transfer to and visit the monument “Motamsaro ona” Grieving mother -the first acquaintance with an Uzbek woman. Then a visit to the Museum of Applied Arts.
Tea ceremony
A lesson for "future Uzbek" brides "Make and serve tea to guests according to Uzbek traditions!"
Sounds very easy, like “Give me your cup and here is your!”
But in Uzbekistan, it's not quite that simple and easy. There are centuries-old traditions, special rules that every Uzbek girl, or daughter-in-law, must follow.
A little theory and, of course, practice awaits you.
12:30 - we leave for the Chorsu trade market. You will visit the largest Bazaar not only in the city, but in the whole country. And also, learn how to do the right shopping!
"Learn to bargain before buying!"
After all, according to Uzbek traditions, at the bazaar, you have to bargain. There are certain ways and tricks for better bargaining, your guide will teach you all this.
14:00-15:30 Lunch at "Milliy Taomlar", where women slice Naryn.
16:00 -17:00 - free time
18:00 – visit to the A. Navoi Theatre
21:00 after having delicious dinner at the national restaurant Nurafshon, we go to the hotel for the night.
